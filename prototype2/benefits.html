WISCONSIN NFA
GUN TRUSTS

Benefits

  • A Trust is not required to submit fingerprint cards with a form 1 or form 4
  • A Trust is not required to submit a photograph with a form 1 or a form 4 A
  • Trust does not have to have the Chief Law enforcement Officer's signature
  • Ability to protect your family members from inadvertent possession of an item regulated by the NFA
  • Multiple individuals can be listed as the trustees, allowing more than one person to have legal access to the weapons
  • Flexibility - may be amended to fit your changing needs
  • A Trust may be used to purchase and own multiple NFA regulated weapons
  • A Trust can keep you NFA weapons out of the public probate process
